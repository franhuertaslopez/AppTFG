Light Carbohydrates and Proteins|Quinoa Salad with Grilled Chicken|Ingredients: 100g chicken breast, 50g quinoa, 1 tomato, 1 cucumber, 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt and pepper to taste. Preparation: Cook the quinoa according to package instructions. Grill the chicken breast and cut into strips. Mix the cooked quinoa with chopped tomato and cucumber. Add the chicken, dress with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Serve cold or at room temperature.
Light Carbohydrates and Proteins|Whole Wheat Toast with Avocado and Poached Egg|Ingredients: 1 slice whole wheat bread, 1/2 avocado, 1 egg, salt, pepper, lemon. Preparation: Toast the bread, mash the avocado with salt, pepper, and a few drops of lemon. Cook the poached egg. Spread the avocado on the toast and top with the egg.
